# Publish Blog Posts on Ghost

### Step-by-step
1. Log in to your Ghost admin panel (yourdomain.com/ghost).
2. Click 'New Post' and add your title. Ghost uses a clean editor — write in Markdown or use the visual editor.
3. Structure your post: use H2 and H3 headings, short paragraphs, bullet points, and images to improve readability.
4. Add a featured image: this appears at the top of the post and in social share previews. Use a high-quality, relevant image.
5. Set SEO metadata: click the gear icon > Meta data. Write a custom meta title (under 60 chars) and meta description (under 155 chars).
6. Set the URL slug: keep it short and keyword-rich (e.g., /cold-email-best-practices).
7. Add tags: use 1-3 relevant tags to categorize the post. Tags help with site navigation and internal linking.
8. Set the author: ensure the correct team member is credited.
9. Preview the post: check formatting on desktop and mobile views.
10. Publish: choose to publish immediately or schedule for a specific date and time. After publishing, share the link on LinkedIn, email, and social channels.
