# Send Ghost Newsletters

### Step-by-step
1. In Ghost Settings > Email, configure your newsletter settings: from name, from email, and reply-to address.
2. Set up your email design: choose a header image, accent color, and footer content (social links, unsubscribe).
3. Create a new post and toggle 'Email' in the publish settings to send it as a newsletter.
4. Choose the recipient segment: all subscribers, free members, paid members, or a specific label.
5. Write the newsletter: Ghost posts double as email content. Keep paragraphs short — email renders differently than web.
6. Preview the email: click 'Preview' > 'Email' to see how it will look in inboxes.
7. Send a test email to yourself: verify formatting, links, and images render correctly in your email client.
8. Schedule or send: choose to send immediately with publish, or schedule for optimal send time.
9. Monitor email analytics: check open rates, click rates, and unsubscribes in the Ghost post analytics.
10. Grow your subscriber list: add signup forms to your website, include newsletter CTAs in blog posts, and promote on social media.
