# Set Up a LinkedIn Ads Campaign

### Step-by-step
1. Go to LinkedIn Campaign Manager (business.linkedin.com) and select your ad account (or create one).
2. Install the LinkedIn Insight Tag on your website for conversion tracking: go to Account Assets > Insight Tag and add the JavaScript snippet to your site.
3. Create a Campaign Group to organize related campaigns: name it after the initiative (e.g., 'Q1 2025 - Product Launch').
4. Create a new Campaign within the group. Choose your objective: Brand Awareness, Website Visits, Engagement, Lead Generation, or Conversions.
5. Select your ad format: Single Image, Carousel, Video, Message Ad, or Document Ad.
6. Set your daily/lifetime budget and schedule: start with $50-100/day for testing.
7. Choose your bidding strategy: Automated bidding for simplicity, or Manual CPC/CPM for control.
8. Define your target audience (see linkedin-ads-audience-targeting).
9. Create your ad creative: write headline (under 70 chars), ad copy (under 150 chars for best results), and upload your image or video.
10. Review and launch: double-check targeting, budget, and creative. Start with a small budget and increase once you verify performance.
