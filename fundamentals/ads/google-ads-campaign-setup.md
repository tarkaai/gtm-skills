# Set Up a Google Ads Campaign

### Step-by-step
1. Sign in to Google Ads (ads.google.com) and create a new campaign.
2. Choose your campaign objective: Sales, Leads, Website Traffic, or Brand Awareness.
3. Select campaign type: Search (text ads on Google results), Display (banner ads on websites), or Performance Max (automated across all Google properties).
4. Set your geographic targeting: country, region, city, or radius around a location.
5. Set your daily budget: start with $20-50/day for testing. You can increase once you identify winning keywords and ads.
6. Choose a bidding strategy: 'Maximize Conversions' for automated, or 'Manual CPC' for control. Start automated.
7. Create your first ad group: group ads by theme (e.g., one ad group per product feature or pain point).
8. Add keywords to the ad group (see google-ads-keyword-research).
9. Write your ad: Responsive Search Ads allow up to 15 headlines and 4 descriptions. Google mixes and matches to find the best combinations.
10. Set up conversion tracking (see google-ads-conversion-tracking) before launching so you can measure results from Day 1.
