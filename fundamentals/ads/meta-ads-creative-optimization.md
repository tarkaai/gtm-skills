# Optimize Meta Ad Creative

### Step-by-step
1. Create multiple ad variants: test at least 3-5 creative variations per ad set. Meta's algorithm will optimize toward the best performer.
2. For images: use eye-catching visuals that stop the scroll. Avoid stock photos. Show your product, results, or people. Use contrast and bold colors.
3. For video: keep under 15 seconds for Stories/Reels, under 60 seconds for Feed. Hook in the first 3 seconds. Use captions.
4. Write primary text: lead with the benefit or pain point. Use short paragraphs. Include social proof if you have it.
5. Test different angles: feature-focused, pain-point-focused, social-proof-focused, and story-focused creative.
6. Use UGC-style creative: content that looks like organic posts often outperforms polished ads on Meta.
7. Refresh creative every 2-3 weeks: ad fatigue happens fast on Meta. Monitor frequency (when frequency > 3, refresh).
8. Analyze creative performance: compare CTR, conversion rate, and CPA across variants. Kill underperformers after 500+ impressions.
9. Use Dynamic Creative: upload multiple images, headlines, and descriptions and let Meta mix-and-match combinations.
10. Build a creative testing cadence: launch 3-5 new ad variants every 2 weeks. Scale winners and archive learnings.
