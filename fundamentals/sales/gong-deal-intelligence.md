# Use Gong Deal Intelligence

### Step-by-step
1. In Gong, navigate to Deals to see your pipeline with AI-generated deal scores.
2. Review deal engagement: Gong tracks how many calls, emails, and stakeholders are involved in each deal. More engagement usually means healthier deals.
3. Check risk signals: Gong flags deals where competitors were mentioned, pricing objections were raised, or next steps weren't set.
4. Analyze stakeholder mapping: see which contacts at the target company have been engaged. Multi-threaded deals (3+ contacts) close at higher rates.
5. Use Gong's deal score: the AI assigns a score based on engagement, sentiment, and deal progression. Focus on deals with declining scores.
6. Review call recordings for at-risk deals: listen to the last 2-3 calls and identify where the deal may be stalling.
7. Set up deal board views: create views for 'Deals at Risk', 'Deals Closing This Month', and 'Deals Missing Next Steps'.
8. Sync deal intelligence to your CRM: Gong can push insights and call summaries to Attio or other CRMs.
9. Use deal intelligence in forecast reviews: reference Gong data when discussing pipeline confidence in team meetings.
10. Track win/loss patterns: over time, Gong reveals which conversation patterns correlate with wins — apply these insights to coaching.
