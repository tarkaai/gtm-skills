# YouTube Channel and SEO — Smoke Test

> **Stage:** Marketing → Problem Aware | **Motion:** Founder Social Content | **Channels:** Content, Social

## Overview
Create video content optimized for YouTube search and discovery to reach visual learners and build brand authority, from manual uploads to automated production and AI-driven optimization.

**Time commitment:** 10 hours over 4 weeks
**Pass threshold:** ≥500 views and ≥5 conversions in 4 weeks

---

**Play-specific cost:** Free

## Recommended tools
- **PostHog** (Analytics)
- **OBS** (Video)
- **Loom** (Video)

---

## Instructions

1. Research 10-15 YouTube keywords in your niche using YouTube autocomplete and TubeBuddy; prioritize keywords with 1K-10K monthly searches and low competition.

2. Plan 5 video topics aligned with high-opportunity keywords and your ICP's pain points; create simple outlines with key talking points for each video.

3. Record 5 videos (5-10 minutes each) using Loom, OBS, or smartphone; focus on educational content (how-tos, tutorials, explainers) rather than polish.

4. Edit videos for clarity and pacing using free tools (DaVinci Resolve, iMovie); add intro/outro cards, captions, and simple graphics if time permits.

5. Optimize each video for YouTube SEO: title with target keyword, description (300+ words with keywords and timestamps), tags, custom thumbnail with text overlay.

6. Upload all 5 videos to YouTube; add links in description to your website, relevant blog posts, and signup page.

7. Set up PostHog to track referral traffic from YouTube; create UTM parameters for video links to measure conversions from each video.

8. Set pass threshold: ≥500 total views and ≥5 conversions (signup, demo) from the 5 videos within 4 weeks.

9. Share videos on LinkedIn, Twitter, and relevant communities; embed videos in related blog posts to boost views and SEO.

10. After 4 weeks, analyze YouTube Analytics and PostHog: identify top video by views and watch time; assess click-through rate on video links; if you hit the threshold, document your video production process and SEO tactics and proceed to Baseline; if not, refine topics or improve thumbnails/titles.
