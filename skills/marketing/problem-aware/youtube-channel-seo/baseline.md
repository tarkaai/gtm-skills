# YouTube Channel and SEO — Baseline Run

> **Stage:** Marketing → Problem Aware | **Motion:** Founder Social Content | **Channels:** Content, Social

## Overview
Create video content optimized for YouTube search and discovery to reach visual learners and build brand authority, from manual uploads to automated production and AI-driven optimization.

**Time commitment:** 35 hours over 8 weeks
**Pass threshold:** ≥3,000 views and ≥25 conversions over 8 weeks

## Instructions

1. Expand content calendar to 15-20 videos over 8 weeks; map topics to keyword clusters and stages of the customer journey.

2. Research keywords using TubeBuddy or vidIQ; prioritize topics with search volume 2K-20K, competition score <50, and alignment with your product's value proposition.

3. Create video scripts or detailed outlines for each video; include hook (first 15 seconds), key points, examples, and clear CTA.

4. Record 2-3 videos per week; improve production quality with better lighting, audio (e.g., USB mic), and screen recording setup.

5. Edit videos consistently: add branded intro/outro, captions for accessibility, b-roll or screen recordings, and annotations for key points.

6. Optimize all videos for SEO: keyword-rich titles (under 60 chars), detailed descriptions with chapters, 5-8 relevant tags, and high-contrast thumbnails with faces and text.

7. Build YouTube playlists organized by topic or customer journey stage; use playlists to increase session watch time and improve channel authority.

8. Set up PostHog events for youtube_referral, video_cta_click, and conversion; track which videos drive highest conversion rates.

9. Set pass threshold: ≥3,000 total views and ≥25 conversions over 8 weeks, with average view duration ≥40% of video length.

10. After 8 weeks, analyze YouTube Analytics: identify top 3 videos by views and watch time; assess which topics and formats resonate; if you hit the threshold, document video templates, SEO process, and distribution strategy and proceed to Scalable; if not, improve content quality or refine keyword targeting.
